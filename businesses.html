Chimera Credit Solutions

Credit done properly.

What it does

Chimera Credit Solutions is a licensed money lender, operating under Zambia's Companies Act and Money Lenders Act and regulated by the Ministry of Finance. We provide payroll-secured credit to the formally employed staff of partner employers — under structured agreements negotiated at the employer level, not borrower-by-borrower.

Why this model

The B2B-first structure means our underwriting risk is anchored in the employer relationship, not in individual marketing or consumer recovery practices. For employees, the result is faster approvals, better terms, and a credit experience that runs through their HR — not through a sales agent. For employers, the result is a financial benefit they can offer their staff without any internal credit operations burden.

Who it serves

Mid-sized to large employers in Zambia — corporate, parastatal, NGO, and public-sector — whose formally employed staff are eligible for payroll-secured credit. CCS does not operate direct-to-consumer.

Product surface

  • Salary-based personal loans
  • Civil servant loans (under relevant payroll agreements)
  • Order finance for small businesses

Chimera Premium Poultry Farms

Everything Chicken. Better chicken. Proudly Zambian.

What it does

Chimera Premium Poultry Farms raises mid-market premium broilers — better than the commodity supply that dominates open markets, more accessible than imported premium products. Birds are processed at certified abattoirs, packaged under the Chimera Premium Poultry Farms brand, and held under cold-chain storage to retail standard.

The farm currently supplies live birds to wholesale buyers. Our full branded retail range is in preparation and will launch as cold storage and food-handling certification are completed.

The retail range — coming soon

Processed at certified abattoirs, packaged under our brand, cold-chain maintained:

  • Whole Chicken
  • Chicken Drumsticks (1.0 kg)
  • Chicken Breast Fillets (1.0 kg)
  • Chicken Wings (750 g)
  • Chicken Gizzards (500 g)
  • Chicken Livers (500 g)

Wholesale packs (coming soon):

  • 10 kg, 15 kg, and 20 kg portion packs
  • Cold-chain maintained throughout distribution

Quality standards

Premium Quality · Naturally Raised · Safe & Hygienic · Fresh, Clean, Trusted · Proudly Zambian.

Operational distinction

CPPF is run on data. Every batch is tracked end-to-end on KulimaAI — the farm management platform built by Chimera Technologies. The result is consistent supply, traceable production, and operational discipline that the open-market alternatives structurally cannot match.

Chimera Mineral Exchange

Structured trading. Transparent terms.

What it will do

Chimera Mineral Exchange is a structured trading platform for Zambian gold and emeralds, designed to bring transparent pricing, quality verification, and fair commercial terms to artisanal and small-scale producers. CME will operate licensed trading centres in Zambia's key mining regions — engaging directly with producers and serving institutional and international buyers under formal procurement mandates.

Why this model

Zambia is one of the world's significant sources of high-quality emeralds and a growing gold producer. The artisanal and small-scale producers who supply much of this output have historically been disadvantaged by opaque pricing, unverified grading, and informal trading channels.

CME is structured to change that — with calibrated weighing and grading on-site, transparent pricing referenced to international benchmarks, and licensed export channels that meet international ethical-sourcing standards.

Operating model

  • On-site producer registration and verification
  • Calibrated weighing and gemological / metallurgical grading
  • Transparent reference pricing
  • Licensed procurement for international buyer mandates
  • Adherence to ethical-sourcing standards

Chimera Technologies

Software for African agribusiness.

What it does

Chimera Technologies is the group's technology subsidiary, building data-driven software for African agribusiness. We are not a contract software shop. We build products — purpose-built for specific operator categories in African agriculture — and we run them as long-term software businesses.

First product — KulimaAI

KulimaAI is a farm management platform for commercial broiler operators in Zambia. Built by operators who run a working broiler farm, KulimaAI is designed to address the structural data-integrity problem that limits AI-driven farm tooling today: confident recommendations on bad input data are dangerous. KulimaAI is built around a data-integrity gate that other tools structurally lack.

Status

Chimera Technologies is in active development. KulimaAI is in Wave 0 dogfooding at Chimera Premium Poultry Farms (June 2026), with Wave 1 external farmer onboarding scheduled for July 2026 and public commercial launch targeted October 2026.

Beyond KulimaAI

Chimera Technologies will extend into additional African agribusiness software products as KulimaAI proves its model. Each future product will follow the same operator-led design discipline.

How They Connect

Separate companies.
Shared standards.

The four Chimera businesses are deliberately structured as separate companies — separate teams, separate P&Ls, separate balance sheets. But they share a common operating philosophy and a common discipline. The result is real synergies — without the muddled accountability that plagues conglomerates run as single companies.

i.

KulimaAI runs Chimera Premium Poultry Farms

The first customer of Chimera Technologies' KulimaAI product is Chimera Premium Poultry Farms. The internal dogfooding relationship ensures the software is built for real operational reality, not designed in a vacuum. Both businesses benefit — CPPF gets a custom-fitted operations platform; Chimera Technologies gets a working farm as a continuous design partner.

ii.

Shared brand credibility

Each Chimera subsidiary enters its market with the credibility of the group's combined operational standards. A new customer talking to Chimera Credit Solutions, Chimera Premium Poultry Farms, or any subsidiary can verify the group's seriousness across multiple verticals — not just one.

iii.

Shared governance discipline

The same standards of regulatory compliance, financial discipline, and operational integrity apply across every Chimera business. New subsidiaries inherit these standards from day one — they do not have to invent them.
