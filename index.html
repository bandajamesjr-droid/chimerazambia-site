How We Operate

An operating group,
not a fund.

We do not raise outside capital to deploy and exit. We build companies we intend to own and run. That single distinction shapes how we choose sectors, hire teams, and measure success.

i.

Operate, don't speculate

Every Chimera business is run, not flipped. We measure ourselves on whether the underlying company is genuinely better each year — in product quality, in margin, in team capability, in customer experience. A company we are not prepared to own for two decades is not a company we are prepared to own at all.

ii.

Discipline over scale

We would rather run one excellent company than three mediocre ones. Each vertical earns the right to grow by proving its unit economics, its operational rigour, and its team. Chimera Credit Solutions launched when the underwriting discipline was in place. Premium Poultry Farms launched when the operational systems could support the brand standard. Mineral Exchange and Technologies will launch the same way.

iii.

Build for Zambia first

Our businesses are designed for the realities of the Zambian market — the regulatory environment, the customer base, the supplier and distribution landscape. Built that way, they are engineered to a standard that travels beyond Zambia when the time is right. We do not import templates and force them to fit. We build from first principles, here.

Our Progress

A short
history.

Chimera has grown deliberately — adding a vertical only once the previous one was on a sound operational footing.

2022

The group is founded, and Chimera Credit Solutions launches

Chimera Investment Group Ltd is incorporated in Lusaka under the Companies Act 2017 as a privately held operating company. In the same year, the group's first operating business — Chimera Credit Solutions — begins providing payroll-secured credit through structured agreements with employers, a B2B model that anchors lending in the employer relationship rather than individual marketing.

2025

Chimera Premium Poultry Farms begins production

The group enters commercial agriculture with a broiler operation built around the mid-market premium positioning — better than commodity, more accessible than imported premium.

2026

Two new verticals enter development

Chimera Mineral Exchange and Chimera Technologies move into active development — extending the group into structured resource trading and African agribusiness software. KulimaAI enters Wave 0 dogfooding at Chimera Premium Poultry Farms.
