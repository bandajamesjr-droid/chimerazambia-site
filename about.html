The Group

An operating group, built in Zambia, for the long term.

Chimera Investment Group is not an investment fund. We do not raise outside capital to deploy and exit. We are an operating group — a privately held company that owns and runs businesses across the sectors that build a national economy, intending to do so for decades.

Why an Operating Group

The constraint in Zambia is not capital.
It is operators.

Across the sectors that should be Zambia's strongest, the bench of disciplined, long-term, professionally run businesses remains thin.

Capital is available. Market opportunity is available. Government appetite for serious commercial activity is available. What is scarce, structurally, is operators willing to build at quality and for the long term.

Chimera was structured to address that scarcity directly. Rather than building a single company, or operating as a private equity fund that deploys and exits, the group was designed to hold and operate businesses across multiple sectors. Each vertical is chosen because it rewards operational rigour and punishes shortcuts.

Each company is built to compete on quality, run with discipline, and answerable to its own customers. We do not subsidise weak operations from strong ones. We do not chase scale ahead of capability.

The model is not new. Across emerging markets, the diversified operating group — patient ownership of the right businesses, run rather than traded — has repeatedly proven to be how durable national economies get built. We are applying that model to Zambia, from first principles and from the beginning.

Operating Philosophy

Three principles, applied across
every Chimera business.

These are not aspirations. They are operating rules — applied to every subsidiary, every team, every commercial decision.

i.

Operate, don't speculate

Every Chimera business is run, not flipped. We are not a fund with a five-year exit horizon. We measure ourselves on whether the underlying company is genuinely better each year — in product quality, in margin, in team capability, in customer experience. A company we are not prepared to own for two decades is not a company we are prepared to own at all. This is not patience for its own sake. It is the only time horizon long enough for the way we operate to compound.

ii.

Discipline over scale

We would rather run one excellent company than three mediocre ones. Each Chimera vertical earns the right to grow by proving its unit economics, its operational rigour, and its team's capability before any expansion capital is committed. We launched Chimera Credit Solutions only when our underwriting and employer-partnership standards were in place. Chimera Premium Poultry Farms launched when the operational systems could support the standard the brand promises. Chimera Mineral Exchange and Chimera Technologies will launch the same way. Growth that outpaces operational capability is not growth — it is debt against the brand.

iii.

Build for Zambia first

Our businesses are designed for the realities of the Zambian market — the regulatory environment, the customer base, the supplier and distribution landscape, the capital constraints. Built that way, they are then engineered to a standard that travels beyond Zambia when the time is right. We do not import templates from other markets and force them to fit. We build from first principles, here. The result is businesses that serve Zambian customers properly first, and that have the operational foundations to expand regionally when the underlying business is ready.

What We Hold Ourselves To

Group standards,
applied everywhere.

Three standards apply across every Chimera business, regardless of sector. They are non-negotiable — and they apply equally to a four-year-old subsidiary as to one in development.

Standard 1

Regulatory and legal compliance

Every Chimera subsidiary is properly registered, properly licensed, and properly tax-compliant. Chimera Credit Solutions operates as a licensed money lender under Zambia's Companies Act and Money Lenders Act, regulated by the Ministry of Finance. Chimera Premium Poultry Farms operates under PACRA registration, raising premium broilers processed at certified abattoirs. New subsidiaries are not permitted to commence commercial operations until their regulatory position is fully resolved.

Standard 2

Financial discipline

Each Chimera business maintains its own books, its own profit-and-loss accountability, and its own balance sheet. We do not cross-subsidise operationally weak businesses with stronger ones. A company that cannot stand on its own unit economics over a reasonable runway does not belong in the group. This is not financial conservatism — it is the only way to know whether a business is genuinely working.

Standard 3

Data and operational integrity

Every Chimera business operates on the principle that the numbers must be true before they are interpreted. The lessons we have learned building KulimaAI — that confident decisions on bad data are actively dangerous — apply across every subsidiary. Operational decisions are made on verified data, not on assumption. This applies to a credit risk decision at CCS as much as to a flock health decision at CPPF.

Our Progress

Building deliberately,
one vertical at a time.

Chimera has grown without rushing — adding a vertical only once the previous one was on a sound operational footing. The pattern below shows how the group has been built.

2022

The group is founded, and Chimera Credit Solutions launches

Chimera Investment Group Ltd is incorporated in Lusaka under the Companies Act 2017. The group is structured as a private limited company, wholly held by its founder, with a mandate to build businesses across multiple sectors.

In the same year, the group's first operating business — Chimera Credit Solutions — begins providing payroll-secured credit through structured agreements with employers. The B2B-first model, where CCS partners with employers rather than disbursing direct to individuals, emerges from a deliberate decision to build a lending business that does not depend on consumer marketing or aggressive recovery practices.

2025

Chimera Premium Poultry Farms begins production

The group enters commercial agriculture with a broiler operation built around a mid-market premium positioning — better than the commodity supply that floods Lusaka's open markets, more accessible than imported premium chicken.

The full retail packaging line is designed in this period; cold-chain infrastructure and certified-abattoir partnerships become the operational priority for the branded-retail expansion.

2026

Two new verticals enter development

Chimera Mineral Exchange and Chimera Technologies move into active development. CME is structured around transparent gold and emerald trading with artisanal and small-scale producers.

Chimera Technologies launches its first product, KulimaAI — a farm management platform for commercial broiler operators, designed by operators who run a working broiler farm. KulimaAI enters Wave 0 dogfooding at Chimera Premium Poultry Farms.
